Why us

Healthcare clinic operations have been plagued with inefficient staffing deployment, poor room utilization and lack of visibility of day-to-day clinic operations that leads to uncaptured revenue and inadequate care coordination.

OrangeHealth streamlines and optimizes these operations with digital processes so that healthcare staff can focus on the work that matters.

Features

Optimized Scheduling

Automate coverage.

Increase scheduling efficiency by creating templates that maximize employees' preferences and clinic room utilization.

Get real-time room utilization impact when changes are made to the schedule.

Dynamic Clinic Board

Autogenerate provider - allied health rooming assignments with one click.

Identify providers who require additional allied health support.

Automatically reflect unexpected changes in the schedule such as a sick call and provide alternate solutions.

Efficient Request Management

Optimize request approval/rejection process through the auto-approval feature and through increased communication via an automated sms-and-email system.

Allow employees to submit requests via text-messages and ask our intelligent system questions like: "Can I take PTO next Thursday?"

Reports

Learn and predict patterns to allow tasks such as panel management time to be efficciently allocated.

Track clinic room utilization performance.

Gain insights into clinic's performance by matching human and capital resources.
